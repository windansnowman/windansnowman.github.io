**Authors**: Yilong Yang, Xinjing Liu, Zefeng Wu, Zhuoran Ma, Yong Zeng, Xianjia Meng, Zhuo Ma

**Contribution**: First-student author

**Summary**: ReBA assigns higher update weights to durable backdoor-related parameters (via Fisher information) and adaptively tunes amplification to keep malicious updates within benign distance space. Experiments on three datasets and 14 defenses show superior attack success rate and substantially longer persistence, even with only one malicious client.
