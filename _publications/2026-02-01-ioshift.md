**Authors**: Yilong Yang, Zefeng Wu, Yang Liu, Boyun Zhang, Weisheng Dong, Bin Xiao, Zhuo Ma

**Contribution**: First-student author

**Summary**: IOShift shows that backdoor implantation causes measurable bias shifts between ID and OOD data. It detects malicious updates with an IOShift score and removes backdoors via adaptive pruning of critical neurons. Experiments on CIFAR-10 and Tiny-ImageNet under non-IID settings outperform strong baselines in TPR, FPR, and ASR reduction.
